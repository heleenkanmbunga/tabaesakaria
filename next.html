why i love you

I love the quiet hum when you're near, a melody I crave,
The way sunlight catches in your hair, a golden, gentle wave.
I love the crinkle by your eyes when a genuine smile takes hold,
And stories only your kind touch can ever truly unfold.

I love the patience in your gaze when my thoughts run wild and free,
The unwavering strength you show, simply by being you, with me.
I love the way you listen close, to worries I barely share,
And the silent understanding that banishes every fear.

I love the comfort in your presence, a harbor from the storm,
The way your hand fits into mine, keeping my spirit warm.
I love the small, forgotten gestures, noticed only by my heart,
The subtle ways you show you care, right from the very start.

I love the shared laughter that escapes, a joyous, soaring sound,
And the peaceful quiet we embrace, when no words need be found.
I love the way you challenge me, to grow beyond my ken,
And the gentle reassurance that I'm enough, again and again.

I love the dreams we build together, brick by hopeful brick,
The future stretching out before us, so vivid, close, and quick.
I love the solace of your voice, a balm upon my soul,
Making fractured pieces whole, making me truly whole.

And though a million reasons dance, beyond my ability to tell,
Each one a silent promise, where my deepest feelings dwell.
It's not just one, or two, or three, but all of them combined,
The quiet, profound beauty of the love in you I find.
